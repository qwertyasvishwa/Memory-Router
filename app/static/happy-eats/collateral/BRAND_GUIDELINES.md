## ‚úçÔ∏è Typography

### Primary Typeface: **Segoe UI**
- **Reason**: Clean, modern, excellent readability
- **Fallbacks**: Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif
- **License**: System font (widely available)

### Hierarchy

#### **Display Text** (Logos, Hero Headlines)
- **Size**: 48-96px
- **Weight**: 900 (Black/Heavy)
- **Color**: Gradient (#2c2416 ‚Üí #4f8b2c)
- **Usage**: Brand name, major headlines

#### **Headings** (H1-H3)
- **Size**: 24-36px
- **Weight**: 800 (Extra Bold)
- **Color**: #2c2416
- **Usage**: Section headers, product names

#### **Body Text**
- **Size**: 14-16px
- **Weight**: 400 (Regular)
- **Color**: #5c5244
- **Line Height**: 1.6-1.8
- **Usage**: Descriptions, paragraphs

#### **Small Text** (Labels, Badges)
- **Size**: 10-12px
- **Weight**: 700 (Bold)
- **Color**: White on colored backgrounds
- **Usage**: Quality badges, tags

### Typography Guidelines
- **DO**: Use bold weights for emphasis
- **DO**: Maintain generous line spacing (1.6-1.8)
- **DO**: Use sentence case for most text
- **DON'T**: Use all caps except for badges/labels
- **DON'T**: Use decorative or script fonts

## üè∑Ô∏è Logo Usage

### Logo Variations
1. **Primary Logo**: Full wordmark with gradient
2. **White Logo**: For dark backgrounds
3. **Icon Only**: Leaf-heart symbol (standalone)

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "H" in "Happy" on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- **Print**: 1 inch width minimum
- **Digital**: 120px width minimum

### What NOT to Do
- ‚ùå Stretch or distort logo
- ‚ùå Change colors outside brand palette
- ‚ùå Add effects (shadows, outlines, glows)
- ‚ùå Rotate logo
- ‚ùå Place on busy backgrounds

## üí¨ Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Warm**: Like a grandmother sharing recipes
- **Trustworthy**: Honest, transparent, genuine
- **Knowledgeable**: Expert on Indian cuisine and wellness
- **Approachable**: Friendly, never pretentious
- **Nostalgic**: Evoking happy food memories

### Tone by Context

#### **Product Descriptions**
- Warm, descriptive, sensory-rich
- *Example*: "Golden, crispy khakhra kissed with aromatic fenugreek‚Äîa bite of Gujarat's morning tradition."

#### **Health/Wellness Content**
- Informative, reassuring, evidence-based
- *Example*: "Made with 100% whole wheat flour and zero trans fat, our khakhra supports your wellness journey."

#### **Social Media**
- Conversational, engaging, culturally rooted
- *Example*: "Chai time just got crunchier! üçµ‚ú® Which flavor are you dunking today?"

#### **Customer Service**
- Empathetic, helpful, solution-oriented
- *Example*: "We're sorry to hear that! Let's make this right. Can you share more details?"

### Language Guidelines
- **DO**: Use sensory language (crispy, aromatic, golden)
- **DO**: Reference Indian culture and traditions
- **DO**: Speak in second person ("you," "your")
- **DON'T**: Use jargon or complex terms
- **DON'T**: Make exaggerated claims
- **DON'T**: Use negative framing

## üì∏ Photography Style

### Subject Matter
- **Hero Shots**: Products in natural settings (wood surfaces, cotton cloth)
- **Lifestyle**: Families sharing snacks, chai time moments
- **Ingredients**: Close-ups of wheat, spices, fresh ingredients
- **Process**: Hands crafting products (artisanal feel)

### Visual Style
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm, golden hour feel
- **Colors**: Warm tones, avoid cool blues
- **Composition**: Clean, uncluttered, product-focused
- **Props**: Traditional Indian elements (brass, wood, fabric)

### Photography Guidelines
- **DO**: Show products in context (with chai, family)
- **DO**: Highlight texture and craftsmanship
- **DO**: Use natural lighting or warm artificial light
- **DON'T**: Use sterile white backgrounds
- **DON'T**: Over-edit or add artificial colors
- **DON'T**: Show products in non-food contexts
